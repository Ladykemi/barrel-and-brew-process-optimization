# 🍺 Barrel & Brew Process Optimization Project

## 📖 Project Overview
This Business Analysis project focuses on improving operational efficiency within Barrel & Brew, a growing craft brewery facing production delays, inventory inaccuracies, and workflow inefficiencies due to manual processes and outdated equipment.

The project includes a Business Requirements Document (BRD), gap analysis, AS-IS and TO-BE process modelling, functional requirements analysis, and process improvement recommendations.


## 🎯 Business Problem
Barrel & Brew experienced several operational challenges, including:
- Manual demand forecasting
- Inventory inaccuracies
- Production delays
- Equipment downtime
- Inconsistent quality control
- Poor communication between departments
- Staff burnout caused by inefficient workflows

These issues negatively impacted scalability, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction.


## 💡 Proposed Solution
Recommended implementing an integrated digital operational management solution to:
- Automate demand forecasting
- Digitize inventory and recipe management
- Improve quality control processes
- Enable real-time inventory visibility
- Improve communication between teams
- Reduce bottling equipment downtime

## 🛠 Business Analysis Techniques Used
- Business Requirements Documentation (BRD)
- Gap Analysis
- AS-IS Process Analysis
- TO-BE Process Modelling
- Fishbone Analysis
- Functional Requirements Analysis
- Non-Functional Requirements (NFRs)
- Workflow Optimization
